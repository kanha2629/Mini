ABOUT SAHAY

Sahay is a website made for direct use by any established NGO. We as a team thought about the sufferings of the people as well as of those who wished to help them but cannot. Sahay allows you to do the same effortlessly. It makes you aware of the work a certain NGO is doing and about how your donations bring smiles to the world.

A non-government organization or NGO is an independent organization that functions away from the government. Technology has been a vital part of the pandemic. Many works can be easily done with a few clicks because of the technology. We thought technology can work favorably for the NGOs too. We as a team made a website SAHAY for the NGOs to display their work in front of the entire world.

The main objective behind developing this system is to make the process of The NGO-PS provide information about all signed-up VOs / NGOs, and funding with the voluntary sector. This system is designed with all features which are very useful for NGO employees, and management teams. It will maintain the history of the record of NGOs who have applied after or before the schemes. It will maintain the payment details of grants received towards schemes.
